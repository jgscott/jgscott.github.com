### My background

I'm on the faculty of the University of Texas at Austin, in the [Department of Statistics and Data Science](https://www.stat.utexas.edu/) in the College of Natural Sciences and the [McCombs School of Business](https://www.mccombs.utexas.edu/). From 2026 I have been serving as Chair of the Department of Statistics and Data Sciences.  

I collaborate with scholars in a wide range of fields.  [My recent projects]({{ site.baseurl }}/research/) have been in health care, security, infectious disease, and neuroscience.  In the past I’ve also done work in linguistics, political science, finance, astronomy, management, and molecular biology.   I also research various topics in statistical theory and methods, mainly in the area of Bayesian inference. 

I also [teach classes]({{ site.baseurl }}/teaching/) in statistical modeling, data science, and probability for several different groups of students at UT.
