About

I work mainly in the area of Bayesian inference. My current methodological research spans several areas: (1) data-augmentation schemes for Bayesian computation; (2) scalability of algorithms for posterior inference; (3) model selection and multiple testing; (4) prior choice in Bayesian shrinkage estimation; and (5) Bayesian methods in machine learning. My recent collaborative projects have involved linguistics, political science, corporate strategy, finance, neuroscience, infectious disease, astronomy, and molecular biology.
